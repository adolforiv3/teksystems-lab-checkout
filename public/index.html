Transfers

Move or borrow stock between labs. A transfer only ever leaves the lab that actually owns it once that lab's own admin approves - sending to another lab still needs them to accept, and requesting from another lab never lets you pull from their shelf directly: they choose exactly what (and how much) to fulfill it with, or deny it.

Kits

Bundle items that get checked out together often (a field repair kit, a starter toolkit) into one quick-add for the checkout screen. A kit is just a shortcut for building a checkout — every item in it still keeps its own quantity, notes, and return status once it's checked out.
